Kheer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter whole milk
  • 1/2 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup mixed nuts (cashews, almonds, pistachios), chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A few strands of saffron
  • 2 tablespoons raisins

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring the milk to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Wash the basmati rice under cold water and drain it.
  3. Add the washed rice to the boiling milk and simmer on low heat for about 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally. The rice should become soft and the milk will thicken.
  4. Add sugar, cardamom powder, saffron strands, chopped nuts, and raisins to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  5. Continue to cook the kheer for another 10-15 minutes until the sugar dissolves and the flavors blend together.
  6. Remove from heat and let the kheer cool to room temperature.
  7. Refrigerate the kheer for a couple of hours before serving.
  8. Serve chilled and garnish with additional nuts and saffron strands, if desired.

Enjoy the creamy and delicious kheer!
