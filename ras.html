Rasgulla Recipe

Rasgulla Recipe

  • s1 liter whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A few strands of saffron (optional)

    • Instructions:

    1. In a large pot, bring the milk to a boil over medium heat.
    2. Once the milk starts boiling, reduce the heat to low and add the lemon juice. Stir gently until the milk curdles and the whey separates from the curds. This process may take a few minutes.
    3. Line a sieve or colander with a cheesecloth or muslin cloth. Pour the curdled milk into the cloth to strain out the whey. Rinse the curds under cold water to remove any lemony taste.
    4. Gently gather the edges of the cloth and squeeze out any excess water from the curds. Hang the cloth with the curds for about 30 minutes to drain any remaining liquid.
    5. After 30 minutes, remove the curds from the cloth and transfer them to a clean surface. Knead the curds with the heel of your hand until they come together and form a smooth dough-like consistency. This may take about 5-10 minutes.
    6. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a smooth, crack-free ball. Ensure there are no cracks, as they can cause the rasgullas to break during cooking.
    7. In a large pot, combine the sugar, water, cardamom powder, and saffron strands (if using). Bring the mixture to a boil, stirring until the sugar dissolves completely.
    8. Gently slide the rasgulla balls into the boiling sugar syrup. Cover the pot and let them cook over medium heat for about 15 minutes.
    9. After 15 minutes, remove the pot from the heat and let the rasgullas cool down to room temperature.
    10. Once cooled, refrigerate the rasgullas for at least 2 hours before serving. They taste best when chilled.

    Enjoy the delicious homemade rasgullas!

    Kheer Recipe

    Kheer Recipe

    Ingredients:

    • 1 liter whole milk
    • 1/2 cup basmati rice
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1/4 cup mixed nuts (cashews, almonds, pistachios), chopped
    • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
    • A few strands of saffron
    • 2 tablespoons raisins

    Instructions:

    1. In a large pot, bring the milk to a boil over medium heat.
    2. Wash the basmati rice under cold water and drain it.
    3. Add the washed rice to the boiling milk and simmer on low heat for about 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally. The rice should become soft and the milk will thicken.
    4. Add sugar, cardamom powder, saffron strands, chopped nuts, and raisins to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
    5. Continue to cook the kheer for another 10-15 minutes until the sugar dissolves and the flavors blend together.
    6. Remove from heat and let the kheer cool to room temperature.
    7. Refrigerate the kheer for a couple of hours before serving.
    8. Serve chilled and garnish with additional nuts and saffron strands, if desired.

    Enjoy the creamy and delicious kheer!

    Tiramisu Recipe

    Tiramisu Recipe

    Ingredients:

    • 6 egg yolks
    • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 2/3 cup milk
    • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
    • 8 ounces mascarpone cheese
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
    • 24-28 ladyfingers
    • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder

    Instructions:

    1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the egg yolks and sugar together until thick and pale yellow in color.
    2. In a saucepan, heat the milk until it just begins to boil. Remove from heat and gradually add it to the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
    3. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon. Remove from heat and let it cool.
    4. In a separate mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream until soft peaks form.
    5. Add the mascarpone cheese and vanilla extract to the cooled egg mixture. Mix until well combined and smooth.
    6. Gently fold in the whipped cream into the egg and mascarpone mixture until fully incorporated.
    7. Dip each ladyfinger into the cooled coffee and arrange them in a single layer in a rectangular serving dish.
    8. Spoon half of the cream mixture over the ladyfingers and spread it evenly.
    9. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining cream mixture.
    10. Sift cocoa powder over the top of the tiramisu to cover it completely.
    11. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.

    Enjoy the irresistible taste of homemade tiramisu!

    Carbonara Pasta Recipe

    Carbonara Pasta Recipe

    Ingredients:

    • 8 ounces spaghetti or linguine pasta
    • 4 ounces pancetta or bacon, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
    • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)

    Instructions:

    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
    2. In a large skillet, cook the diced pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the pancetta/bacon from the skillet and set aside, leaving the fat in the skillet.
    3. Add the minced garlic to the skillet with the pancetta/bacon fat and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
    4. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper.
    5. Remove the skillet from heat and add the cooked pasta. Toss the pasta in the pancetta/bacon fat and garlic until well coated.
    6. Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta. Quickly toss the pasta with the mixture, ensuring that the heat of the pasta cooks the eggs without scrambling them. The sauce should become creamy and coat the pasta.
    7. Add the cooked pancetta/bacon back to the skillet and toss with the pasta.
    8. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley, if desired.
    9. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious Carbonara pasta!
    Margherita Pizza Recipe

    Margherita Pizza Recipe

    Ingredients:

    • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
    • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
    • A handful of fresh basil leaves
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat the oven to the temperature specified on the pizza dough packaging or to 450°F (230°C) if using homemade dough.
    2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
    3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    5. Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
    6. Sprinkle dried oregano, salt, and pepper over the cheese.
    7. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza.
    8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
    9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
    10. Tear the fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the cooked pizza.
    11. Slice the Margherita pizza and serve hot.
    Gulab Jamun Recipe

    Gulab Jamun Recipe

    Ingredients:

    • 1 cup milk powder
    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
    • 1/4 cup milk, plus extra as needed
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1 cup water
    • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
    • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
    • Oil or ghee for deep frying

    Instructions:

    1. In a mixing bowl, combine the milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking powder.
    2. Add ghee to the bowl and mix well until the mixture resembles bread crumbs.
    3. Gradually add milk and knead the mixture into a soft dough. Add more milk if needed, a little at a time, until the dough is smooth and pliable.
    4. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a smooth ball, ensuring there are no cracks.
    5. In a deep pan or pot, heat oil or ghee for deep frying over medium heat.
    6. While the oil/ghee is heating, prepare the sugar syrup. In a separate saucepan, combine sugar and water and bring to a boil. Stir until the sugar dissolves completely.
    7. Reduce the heat and add cardamom powder and saffron strands (if using) to the sugar syrup. Simmer for a few minutes until the syrup slightly thickens. Remove from heat and keep warm.
    8. Once the oil/ghee is hot, carefully drop the prepared dough balls into the oil/ghee, a few at a time. Fry them on low to medium heat until they turn golden brown on all sides. Make sure to adjust the heat to prevent them from burning.
    9. Remove the fried gulab jamuns using a slotted spoon and transfer them to the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for at least 30 minutes to an hour, allowing the gulab jamuns to absorb the syrup and become soft.
    10. Serve the gulab jamuns warm or at room temperature. They can be enjoyed on their own or with a scoop of ice cream.
